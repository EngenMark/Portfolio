UPL20V - Uni Print Lab

A machine for inspection of collective packaging on the production line. UPL20V automatically weighs cartons with products, applies labels, verifies barcode correctness, and rejects cartons that do not meet required criteria.

Details, photos, video

EP10 – Enhanced Palletizing System

A machine for automatic stacking of cartons on two pallets. EP10 receives cartons from the conveyor, groups them in layers, and places them on one of two palletizing stations.

Details, photos, video

RC50 – ROPP Cupping Machine

A machine designed for capping bottles with aluminum ROPP caps and clinching atomizers.

Details, photos, video

RV60 – Vision Inspection System

A machine designed for quality control of markings and labeling on bottles and jars (both round and flat). RV60 verifies the correctness of printed date and batch number, as well as the angle and height of label application.

Details, photos

UO60 - Inkjet Rotator

A machine designed for inkjet printing on jars and bottles. UO60 positions the packaging correctly based on the previously scanned barcode.

Details, photos

KV4 - Vacuum Chamber

A device designed for structural saturation of mushrooms with water or preserving solutions to specific technological parameters.
