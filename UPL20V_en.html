My achievements in the UPL20V – Uni Print Lab project

As part of the UPL20V project, I was responsible for developing a new version of the carton labeling machine. The previous design applied the label to the side of the carton; my task was to design a solution that applies the label from the top, wrapping it partially onto the front wall of the carton.

The machine was designed completely from scratch – with a focus on simplifying the construction. Compared to the previous version, which was enclosed in a separate cabin with polycarbonate covers, the new version has a compact, open structure.

I also designed a carton guiding mechanism that, using a single electric actuator, synchronously moves two belts towards the center. This ensures that the label is always applied centrally – regardless of the carton’s width.

Additionally, the machine was designed to minimize the time needed for programming by automation engineers. Only two parameters are used in recipes: carton width and label type.
