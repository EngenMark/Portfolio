My achievements in the EP10 project – Enhanced Pelletizing System

As part of the EP10 project, I was responsible for redesigning an existing palletizer. The company already had a previous version, but due to its limitations – mainly the overall height of 4.3 m – a complete rebuild was necessary.

I designed a completely new version of the machine with a telescopic Z-axis, which reduced the total height of the machine to 3.2 m without losing functionality. This made the system much more suitable for production halls with limited space.

In addition to the new axis, I also designed:

  • a new conveyor receiving cartons from the line,
  • new pallet placement stations,
  • sliding safety doors,
  • and integrated LED panels for light signaling at each palletizing station.

Although the project was originally planned as a modernization, in practice it resulted in a completely new machine – designed from scratch and adapted to modern production requirements.

This was the company’s first project of this type, which makes EP10 an important engineering achievement confirming my competences in designing advanced special-purpose machines.
